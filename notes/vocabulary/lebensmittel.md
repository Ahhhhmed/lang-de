### Quantities & Packaging (Mengen & Verpackungen)
- **die Portion** = portion / serving
- **das Stück** = piece
- **das Paket / das Päckchen** = packet / parcel / small pack
- **die Packung / die Schachtel** = package / pack / box
- **die Dose / die Büchse** = can / tin
- **der Becher** = cup / tub (e.g. joghurt)
- **die Flasche** = bottle
- **der Kasten** = crate (e.g. of beer)
- **die Tüte** = paper/plastic bag
- **das Kilo (-gramm)** = kilogram
- **das Pfund** = pound (500g)
- **das Gramm** = gram
- **der Liter / der Deziliter** = liter / deciliter
