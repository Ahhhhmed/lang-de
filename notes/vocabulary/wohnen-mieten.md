### Key Locations & Measurements
- **das Stadtzentrum** = city center
- **die Vorstadt** = suburbs
- **der Stadtrand** = outskirts
- **eine Wohnung in der Stadt** = an apartment in the city
- **eine Wohnung auf dem Land** = an apartment in the country
- **die Fläche** = surface area / space
- **der Quadratmeter (m²)** = square meter
- **inklusiv (inkl.)** = inclusive (e.g. rent including utility costs)
