# ⏱️ Daily Routine (Tagesablauf) & Sentence Connectors

This text practices vocabulary for a daily routine while highlighting sequential connectors and the **V2 rule** (Verb must occupy Position 2 in a main clause, causing inversion with the subject).

---

### 💬 Routine Paragraph (Tagesablauf)

*   <span style="text-decoration: underline;">Zuerst</span> wachst du <span style="text-decoration: underline;">auf</span>. *(First, you wake up)*
*   <span style="text-decoration: underline;">Dann</span> frühstückst du. *(Then, you eat breakfast)*
*   <span style="text-decoration: underline;">Danach</span> räumst du den Tisch <span style="text-decoration: underline;">ab</span>. *(After that, you clear the table)*
*   <span style="text-decoration: underline;">Anschliessend</span> wäschst du <span style="text-decoration: underline;">ab</span>. *(Then, you wash the dishes)*
*   Im Anschluss trockn<span style="color: red; text-decoration: underline;">e</span>st du <span style="text-decoration: underline;">ab</span>. *(Following that, you dry the dishes)*
*   Dann duschst du. *(Then, you shower)*
*   Danach <span style="text-decoration: underline;">ziehst</span> du <span style="text-decoration: underline;">dich an</span>. *(After that, you get dressed - reflexive)*
*   <span style="text-decoration: underline;">Am Ende (vom Tag)</span> ziehst du dich <span style="text-decoration: underline;">aus</span>. *(At the end of the day, you undress)*
*   Schlussendlich schläfst du <span style="text-decoration: underline;">ein</span>. *(Finally, you fall asleep)*

---

### 🚦 Word Order & Inversion Rules

When a time connector (adverb) starts the sentence, it occupies **Position 1**. The verb **must** occupy **Position 2**, pushing the subject to **Position 3** (Inversion).

#### Examples of Correct vs. Incorrect Structure:
*   `... frühstückst du und du räumst...` *(Correct: "und" is Position 0, so "du" is Pos 1, "räumst" is Pos 2)*
*   ❌ `... und <span style="text-decoration: line-through;">du räumst</span>...` *(Incorrect when using time connectors)*
*   ✅ `... danach`<sup>[Pos 1]</sup> `räumst`<sup>[Pos 2]</sup> `du`<sup>[Pos 3]</sup> ...
*   ❌ `... räumst danach...` *(Incorrect verb-first order for statement)*
*   ❓ `... räumst du (?)` *(This would be a question, not a statement)*
