# 🎨 Adjective Declension in Dativ (Adj.-Dekl. Dativ)

> **Main Sentence context:** Ich mache einen Snack mit... *(I make a snack with...)*  
> *(Note: The preposition **mit** always demands the **Dativ** case)*

---

### 💡 The Dativ Golden Rule
> [!IMPORTANT]
> In the **Dativ** case, if there is **any article** (definite, indefinite, possessive, or negative) preceding the adjective, the adjective ending is **always `-en`**!

---

### 📊 Summary Reference (Dativ Case Endings)

| Gender | Indefinite Article (`ein-`) | Definite Article (`der-`) | Adjective Ending |
| :--- | :---: | :---: | :---: |
| **Masculine (m)** | `einem` | `dem` | <span style="color: red; font-weight: bold;">-en</span> |
| **Neuter (n)** | `einem` | `dem` | <span style="color: red; font-weight: bold;">-en</span> |
| **Feminine (f)** | `einer` | `der` | <span style="color: red; font-weight: bold;">-en</span> |
| **Plural (pl)** | `keinen` / `meinen` | `den` | <span style="color: red; font-weight: bold;">-en</span> |

---

### 💬 Whiteboard Examples

#### 🔵 Masculine (der Apfel - *apple*)
*   ... mit **einem** frisch<span style="color: red; font-weight: bold;">en</span> Apfel.
*   ... mit **dem** frisch<span style="color: red; font-weight: bold;">en</span> Apfel.

#### 🟢 Neuter (das Gemüse - *vegetable*)
*   ... mit **einem** grün<span style="color: red; font-weight: bold;">en</span> Gemüse.
*   ... mit **dem** grün<span style="color: red; font-weight: bold;">en</span> Gemüse.

#### 🔴 Feminine (die Banane - *banana*)
*   ... mit **einer** reif<span style="color: red; font-weight: bold;">en</span> Banane.
*   ... mit **der** reif<span style="color: red; font-weight: bold;">en</span> Banane.

#### 🟡 Plural (die Früchte - *fruits*)
*   ... mit [ohne] tropisch<span style="color: red; font-weight: bold;">en</span> Früchte<span style="color: red; text-decoration: underline;">n</span>.
*   ... mit **keinen** / **den** tropisch<span style="color: red; font-weight: bold;">en</span> Früchte<span style="color: red; text-decoration: underline;">n</span>.
    *   *(Note: In Dativ Plural, the noun also adds an **-n** ending: Früchte ➔ Früchte**n**)*
