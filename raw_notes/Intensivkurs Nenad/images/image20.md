# 📏 Comparatives & Superlatives

> [Note: Board explains comparison forms with *klein*, plus *als* vs. *wie* and several irregular comparison forms.]

---

### 📊 Regular Pattern

| Positive | Comparative | Superlative |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **klein** | klein<span style="color: red; font-weight: bold;">er</span> | am klein<span style="color: red; font-weight: bold;">sten</span> / der klein<span style="color: red; font-weight: bold;">ste</span> |

Examples:
*   Zürich ist klein.
*   Bern ist klein<span style="color: red; font-weight: bold;">er</span>.
*   Zug ist am klein<span style="color: red; font-weight: bold;">sten</span>.
*   Zug ist klein<span style="color: red; font-weight: bold;">er</span> <span style="color: red; border: 1.5px solid red; border-radius: 50%; padding: 1px 5px;">als</span> Bern.
*   Bern ist klein<span style="color: red; font-weight: bold;">er</span> <span style="color: red; border: 1.5px solid red; border-radius: 50%; padding: 1px 5px;">als</span> Zürich.

---

### ⚖️ *als* vs. *wie*

| Structure | Use | Example |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **... <span style="color: red;">als</span> ...** | different degree | Zug ist kleiner <span style="color: red; border: 1.5px solid red; border-radius: 50%; padding: 1px 5px;">als</span> Bern. |
| **so / gleich / ebenso / genauso ... <span style="color: red;">wie</span> ...** | same degree | Basel ist so gross <span style="color: red; border: 1.5px solid red; border-radius: 50%; padding: 1px 5px;">wie</span> Genf. |

---

### 🔑 Common Irregular Forms

| Positive | Comparative | Superlative |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| gut | <span style="text-decoration: underline;">besser</span> | <span style="text-decoration: underline;">am besten</span> |
| viel / sehr | <span style="text-decoration: underline;">mehr</span> | <span style="text-decoration: underline;">am meisten</span> |
| gern | <span style="text-decoration: underline;">lieber</span> | <span style="text-decoration: underline;">am liebsten</span> |
| nicht gern | <span style="text-decoration: underline;">weniger gern</span> | <span style="text-decoration: underline;">am wenigsten gern</span> |
| warm | wärm<span style="color: red; font-weight: bold;">er</span> | am wärm<span style="color: red; font-weight: bold;">sten</span> |
| gross | gröss<span style="color: red; font-weight: bold;">er</span> | am gröss<span style="color: red; font-weight: bold;">ten</span> |
