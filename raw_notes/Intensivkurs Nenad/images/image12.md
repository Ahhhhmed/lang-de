# 🔄 Two-Way Prepositions (Wechselpräpositionen)

This graphic illustrates the **9 prepositions** that change case based on whether they describe a static location (`+ Dativ`) or a direction of movement (`+ Akkusativ`).

---

### 📊 Case Selection Rule

*   **`+ Dativ`** = **Lokal** (Question: *Wo?* / Where? $\rightarrow$ Static location, no movement across borders)
*   **`+ Akkusativ`** = **Direktional** (Question: *Wohin?* / Where to? $\rightarrow$ Movement towards or into a new state/space)

---
