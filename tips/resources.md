# German Learning Resources

Recommended resources for immersion and practice.

## Audio & Listening

### Deutsche Welle (DW)
- **Langsam Gesprochene Nachrichten** (Slow-paced news): Good for listening practice, even if not easy. Available on the DW website and likely on Spotify/Apple Music.
    - Website: [learngerman.dw.com](https://learngerman.dw.com)
- **Deutschtrainer**: Good content for learning.
    - Website: [learngerman.dw.com](https://learngerman.dw.com)
- **Recommended Articles/Topics**:
    - "Wie politisch ist die Fußball-WM 2026?" (on [learngerman.dw.com](https://learngerman.dw.com))

### Podcasts
- **Easy German Podcast**: [easygerman.org/podcast](https://www.easygerman.org/podcast)
- **Slow German**: [slowgerman.com](https://slowgerman.com/)

## Video (TV Shows & Movies)

- **General Rule**: Always watch in German **WITH subtitles**.
- **Tip**: Find series you already liked and watch them again in High German (Hochdeutsch) with subtitles.
